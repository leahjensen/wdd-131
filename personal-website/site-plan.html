Overview

Purpose

Welcome to my corner of the web—a place where coding, creativity, and fun collide! This site is designed for anyone who wants to learn more about my journey, my projects, and what makes me happy.

Audience

The audience for this site includes friends, family, and anyone curious about who I am and what I create. It's perfect for those interested in coding, design, and getting a glimpse into my creative journey. Whether you're here to connect, find inspiration, or just explore, there's something for everyone!

Dynamic elements

I think I will use JavaScript to make my site interactive and engaging by adding dynamic elements like a theme switcher to toggle between light and dark modes. I’ll also include a personalized greeting that updates based on the time of day and a project showcase section with clickable filters to display different categories of my work.
